# ECONOMICS.md — Unit Economics

## What a Converted Lead is Worth to Credex

A "converted lead" is a startup that books a Credex consultation after the audit and purchases discounted AI credits.

**Assumptions:**
- Average AI spend per startup entering Model-meter: $800/month across all tools
- Average potential savings identified: 25% of spend = $200/month
- Credex margin on discounted credits: ~15% of credit volume sold
- Average credit purchase per converted startup: $500/month (half their current AI spend, switched to Credex)
- Monthly recurring revenue per converted customer to Credex: $500 × 15% = **$75/month**
- Typical contract length: 12 months
- LTV per customer: $75 × 12 = **$900**

This is conservative. Startups that find > $500/month savings (the Credex CTA threshold) have higher budgets and are more likely to purchase credits at scale. The actual LTV skews higher for the high-savings segment.

## CAC at Each GTM Channel

| Channel | Cost | Audits Generated | Lead Rate | Consultations | CAC |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Hacker News (Show HN) | $0 | 300 | 15% | 3 | $0 |
| Reddit posts | $0 | 100 | 15% | 1.5 | $0 |
| Twitter/X thread | $0 | 80 | 12% | 1 | $0 |
| LinkedIn cold DM | ~2hr work | 20 | 25% | 0.5 | ~$50 (time cost) |
| Credex existing pipeline | $0 | varies | 40% | high | $0 |

**Blended CAC at this stage: approximately $10–20 per consultation** (factoring in founder time). If 30% of consultations convert to customers, CAC is $33–67 per customer. At $900 LTV, this is a 13–27x LTV:CAC ratio — very healthy for a SaaS business.

## Conversion Rate Math: Audit → Consultation → Purchase

```
100 audits completed
  → 18 email captures (18% lead rate — conservative for B2B tool)
    → 5 high-savings leads (> $500/month, ~28% of captured leads)
      → 2 consultation bookings (40% of high-savings leads book)
        → 1 purchase (50% of consultations convert)

Revenue from 100 audits: 1 × $900 LTV = $900
Cost per audit (Anthropic Haiku): ~$0.003
Total variable cost for 100 audits: $0.30
```

**Revenue per 100 audits: $900** (at this early conversion funnel)

The funnel tightens significantly in the early days before trust is established. With a 6-month-old product and testimonials, I'd expect the consultation booking rate to reach 60%+ and the purchase rate to reach 70%+, approximately tripling the revenue per audit.

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

**Target:** $1M ARR = $83,333/month in recurring credit revenue

**At $75/month LTV per customer:** Requires 1,111 active customers

**Acquisition math:**
- Month 1–3: Organic (HN, Reddit, Credex pipeline) — 10 customers/month = 30 customers
- Month 4–6: Optimized organic + Credex sales integration — 40 customers/month = 120 customers
- Month 7–12: Content marketing + referral program — 80 customers/month = 480 customers
- Month 13–18: Paid acquisition ($50 CAC, $900 LTV, positive ROI) — 100 customers/month = 600 customers

Total by month 18: 30 + 120 + 480 + 600 = 1,230 customers ≈ **$92K MRR = $1.1M ARR**

This is achievable if: (1) Credex's existing sales team uses Model-meter as a qualification tool from day 1, (2) the organic content strategy produces consistent SEO traffic by month 4, and (3) churn stays below 5% per month (customers stay because AI tooling costs are recurring and re-auditing is useful quarterly).

**The biggest lever:** Credex's existing pipeline. If Credex sends 50 warm leads/month through Model-meter, the path to $1M ARR compresses from 18 months to 12.
