# GTM.md — Go-to-Market Plan

## Exact Target User

**Primary:** Engineering Manager or Technical Co-founder at a Series A startup (30–80 engineers). They received a Rippling or Brex bill this month and AI tools are now in the top 5 line items. Their CFO is asking them to justify the spend in the next all-hands. They are technically literate but don't have time to audit 6 different vendor dashboards manually.

**Secondary:** CTO at a seed-stage startup who put Cursor on the company card for 5 engineers 8 months ago and hasn't reviewed it since. They suspect they're paying for things that overlap.

**Who is NOT the target:** A solo developer curious about their own subscriptions (they have no organizational savings to find). An enterprise procurement team (they have their own audit tooling). A non-technical founder who can't answer "which plan are you on."

## How to Get First 100 Users in 30 Days with $0

**Week 1: Hacker News launch**

Post in "Show HN: I built a free tool that audits AI tool overspend for startups." Hacker News is the highest-leverage single post for developer tools. Target: 50 upvotes, 200 unique visitors, 15 audits completed.

**Week 2: Targeted Reddit posts**

Post in r/ExperiencedDevs and r/startups with a genuine story: "I found $480/month in AI overspend in 20 minutes — here's what I looked for." Include the tool as a resource, not as the lede. Target: 2 posts, 40 unique visitors each.

**Week 3: Direct Twitter/X thread**

Thread: "5 ways engineering teams waste money on AI tools (and how to find yours in 5 minutes)." Each tweet covers one rule from the audit engine with real examples. Pin to profile, tag 2–3 well-followed AI/tooling accounts. Target: 300 impressions minimum, 30 clicks.

**Week 4: Cold DM to 20 EMs on LinkedIn**

Find EMs at Series A startups who've posted about AI productivity tools in the last 90 days. Message: "Noticed you're building with Cursor/Copilot — we built a free audit tool that found $X/month in savings for similar teams. Takes 60 seconds." No ask, just share. Target: 5 responses, 3 audits.

**The "unfair distribution channel":** Credex's existing sales pipeline. Credex already talks to EMs and CTOs about AI credits. Model-meter becomes the top-of-funnel tool that surfaces intent before any sales call. Every existing Credex customer who runs an audit converts to a confirmed lead. This is not public distribution — it's internal distribution that Credex controls entirely.

## Week-1 Traction Targets

- 100 audit completions
- 20 email captures (20% lead rate on audit completions)
- 3 "high-savings" leads (> $500/month) referred to Credex for consultation
- 1 Hacker News post with 50+ upvotes

These numbers are not optimistic. They assume 70% of Show HN viewers leave immediately, 20% try the tool, and 15% complete an audit. 100 completions requires ~3,300 Show HN visitors — achievable for a well-crafted post on a weekday morning.
